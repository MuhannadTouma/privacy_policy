Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Jan 4, 2026 Version: 1.0

Privacy Commitment

Life Manager is built on an offline-first architecture. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

Data Collection

Our application does not collect any personal information. "Personal Information" refers to any data that can identify you, such as your name, email address, location, or device ID. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of digital fingerprinting.

Offline Usage

The Application functions entirely offline. All information you input is stored locally on your device's internal storage. This data is managed by the operating system (Android/iOS) and is never accessible to us.

No User Accounts

We do not require you to create an account to use the Application. Because there are no accounts, we do not have a database of users or their contact information.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any data with third parties. Furthermore, our Application does not contain:

  • Third-party advertisements
  • Analytics SDKs (like Google Analytics or Flurry)
  • Social Media integration hooks
  • External cloud syncing services

Children's Privacy

Our Application is safe for users of all ages. Because we do not collect any personal data, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Policy Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Since the app is offline and we have no way to contact you, we encourage you to review this page periodically. The "Effective Date" at the top will indicate when the last changes were made.
